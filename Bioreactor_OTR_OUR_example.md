# Example 1 — Oxygen Stability in a Stirred Tank Bioreactor

Given:
- kLa = 80 h⁻¹
- C*_O = 8 mg/L
- C_O = 2 mg/L
- q_O = 0.5 mmol/g/h
- X = 5 g/L

## Step 1: Calculate OTR

\[
OTR = k_L a (C_O^* - C_O)
\]

\[
OTR = 80 (8 - 2) = 480
\]

## Step 2: Calculate OUR

\[
OUR = q_O X = 0.5 × 5 = 2.5
\]

## Interpretation:

Since OTR ≫ OUR → Oxygen supply sufficient → Stable regime
