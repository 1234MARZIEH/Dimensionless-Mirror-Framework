# Example 3 — Scale-Up Sensitivity Using Damköhler Number

Lab scale:
Da = 0.3

Pilot scale:
Mixing time increases → Da becomes 2.5

## Interpretation:

Shift from mixed regime to transport-limited regime.

## Risk:
- Concentration gradients
- Oxygen limitation
- Thermal hotspots

## Action:
Increase agitation power or gas flow rate.
