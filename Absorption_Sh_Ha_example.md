# Example 2 — Reactive Absorption Screening

Given:
- Re = 2500
- Sc = 900
- k = 5 s⁻¹
- D_eff = 2×10⁻⁹ m²/s
- k_L = 2×10⁻⁴ m/s

## Step 1: Calculate Hatta Number

\[
Ha = \frac{\sqrt{k D_{eff}}}{k_L}
\]

## Result:
Ha > 3 → Film-controlled reactive absorption

## Engineering Decision:
Increase liquid-side turbulence or packing efficiency.
