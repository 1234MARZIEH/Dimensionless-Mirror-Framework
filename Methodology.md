# Methodological Framework

## 1. Dimensionless Groups for Bioreactors

### Damköhler Number

\[
Da = \frac{r_{char} L}{u_{char}}
\]

Where:
- \( r_{char} \): characteristic reaction rate
- \( L \): characteristic length scale
- \( u_{char} \): characteristic velocity

---

### Volumetric Mass Transfer Coefficient

\[
k_L a \quad [time^{-1}]
\]

---

### Oxygen Balance

\[
OTR = k_L a (C_O^* - C_O)
\]

\[
OUR = q_O X
\]

Stability condition:

\[
OTR \ge OUR
\]

---

## 2. Dimensionless Groups for Gas–Liquid Absorption

### Reynolds Number

\[
Re = \frac{\rho u L}{\mu}
\]

### Schmidt Number

\[
Sc = \frac{\mu}{\rho D_{AB}}
\]

### Sherwood Number

\[
Sh = \frac{k_c L}{D_{AB}}
\]

### Hatta Number

\[
Ha = \frac{\sqrt{k D_{eff}}}{k_L}
\]
